Our Story

Discover the passion behind Gourmet Haven

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Laurent, Gourmet Haven began as a small bistro with just 10 tables. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest local ingredients and let their natural flavors shine through thoughtful preparation.

Michael Laurent
Executive Chef & Founder
